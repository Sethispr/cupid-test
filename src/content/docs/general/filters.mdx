These are the filters used in the bot to make sorting easier, most filters are similar. You can also **combine filters** for more specific results.

## Filter Options

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="=cdex">

- **-a** [ability name, e.g. `nightfall`]
- **-e** [element, e.g. `balance`, `fairy`, `light`, `dark`, etc.]
- **-n** [card name]
- **-s** [series name]

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="=market">

- **-a** [ability name, e.g. `nightfall`]
- **-e** [element, e.g. `balance`, `fairy`, `light`, `dark`, etc.]
- **-m** [maturation number 1 to 3, e.g. 1]
- **-n** [card name]
- **-r** [rarity, e.g. `secr`]
- **-s** [series name]

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="=inv">

- **-a** [ability name, e.g. `nightfall`]
- **-e** [element, e.g. `balance`, `fairy`, `light`, `dark`, etc.]
- **-m** [maturation number 1 to 3, e.g. 1]
- **-n** [card name]
- **-p** [page number]
- **-r** [rarity, e.g. `secr`]
- **-s** [series name]

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="=rd">

- **-a** [ability name, e.g. `nightfall`]
- **-d** [raid difficulty, e.g. `=rd -d Easy`]
- **-e** [element, e.g. `balance`, `fairy`, `light`, `dark`, etc.]
- **-m** [maturation number 1 to 3, e.g. 1]
- **-n** [card name]
- **-r** [rarity, e.g. `secr`]
- **-s** [series name]

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="=cadd">

`=cadd` is used when trading for adding cards.

- **-a** [ability name, e.g. `nightfall`]
- **-e** [element, e.g. `balance`, `fairy`, `light`, `dark`, etc.]
- **-m** [maturation number 1 to 3, e.g. 1]
- **-n** [card name]
- **-r** [rarity, e.g., `secr`]
- **-s** [series name]

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="=select">

`=select` is used for updating cosmetics in your ID/profile.

- **-a** [avatar name]
- **-b** [border name]
- **-badge** [badge name]
- **-bg** [background name]
- **-c** [color hex code, e.g. `#fff271`]
- **-f** [font name]

:::note 

You can also set these filters by clicking the Edit button inside your ID card at [Cupid's Website](https://cupidparadise.top/profile). You cannot combine filters for `=select`.  

:::

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Examples

**Do**:
- `=cdex -e fire, -a armor` (show cards with the fire element and armor piercing ability in the card dex)
- `=inv -r m -mat 3` (shows your mythic maturation 3 cards in `=inv`)
- `=cadd -r secr -s noragami` (adds all your secret rare noragami cards to the trade)

**Don't**:
- `=cdex -e` (missing element)
- `=mk -p 12` (wrong filter, -p filter is only for `=inv`)
- `=select -bg Bond -a Dipper` (cannot combine filters for `=select`)
